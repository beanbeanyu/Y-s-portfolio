p5js Assignment

  1. project1 Lost and Found

    Created a drawing using p5js of my partner's lost wallet in NYC.

  2. project2 Face Generator

    By clicking the mouse or using the keyboard, the background and the protagonist in the work can change in sequence.

  3. project3 Experimental Clock

    This is a clock that shows how your mood changes throughout the day. The circle moving to the right represents the 24 hours of a day. The circles moving up and down represent different emotions. The bottom circle represents a specific emotional value of happiness. Blue for unhappy, green for general, red is happy mood.

  4. project4 Exquisite Corpse

    I coded character designs from three different friends of mine and put them together.

  5. project5 Optical Illusion

    By moving the mouse, the color and shape size can be controlled, thus forming a visual illusion effect.

  6. project6 Data Portrait

    A visualization of my use of different types of apps on my phone over a week.

  7. project7 Autobiographical Game

    This is a 2D fishing game. In the game, players need to use superb fishing skills to complete the set goals and avoid bombs.
